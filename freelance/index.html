WRITING

撒落在太平洋的彩虹群島

(English: The Rainbow Atolls Draped On the Pacific)

Featured in Business Weekly Taiwan 2015.08.19

Driven by curiosity and the passion to share, I documented my remarkable journey to the Marshall Islands, a gem on the Pacific that most people will never visit because of its geological location and lack of tourism development. Discovering the beauty and unique nuances of its people and culture, this piece invites readers to embrace diversity, along with a grander vision to spark their desire to seek for more.
